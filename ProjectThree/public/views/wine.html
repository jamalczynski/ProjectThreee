Space Wine

Space wine was the first place to open up selling wine somewhere other than earth. Shortly after starting, Space Wine became the single larges wine seller in all of the milky way. There best selling wine being Abduction. Soon they plan to expand to other galaxies than just the milky way. Before they can achieve this goal thy must first expand their production facilities. There are so many people buying their wine that they are having trouble keeping their stocks up. Some locations are getting filled with unhappy customers since the wine they desire is sold out. They have started to increase the costs and sell gummy bears with the wine since so many people want the wine and everyone knows that aliens love gummy bears so its just a chance to make even more money. We can expect to see Space Wine universe wide some time in 2230.
