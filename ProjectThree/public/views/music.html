Your Lie in April

Kousei Arima was the main character in an anime know as "Your Lie in April" that first began airing on October 9, 2014. When Kousei was a young boy he learned to play piano from his mother, a well-known pianist throughout japan. After she passed away due to illness Kousei began to realize he could no longer hear notes as he played them on the piano. The world as Kousei knew it became monochrome and he stopped playing the piano. That is, until he met a girl name Kaori Miyazono.

Kaori Miyazono, like Kousei, grew up dreaming of playing the piano. However, she changed her instrument of choice to violin, in hopes of one day being able to play on stage with the great Kousei Arima. In order to fulfill her dream she must first save Kousei from his fears of the past and convince him to take up the piano once again, but no one has been able to get Kousei on a piano in the last 2 years. When Kaori faces a similar fate to that of his mothers, Kousei wishes believes he should quit again, but he plays on because his friend taught him what it truly means to be a pianist.
